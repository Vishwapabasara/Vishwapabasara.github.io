Benthota Turtle Hatchery





About the Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery Project is a favorite of all tourists to Bentota. The lovely golden sands that draw you to our island also provide an enticing nesting place for various endangered turtle species, including the Olive Ridley and Leatherback turtles. The project's goal is to protect and maintain the eggs against predators and other hazards. Visit the turtle hatchery in Bentota to learn more about these magnificent and endangered marine animals.
